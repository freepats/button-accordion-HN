Button accordion HN
-------------------

Version 2024-03-21

Sound bank made with samples of a Hohner accordion recorded by Jeff Stauffer in November 2023, and sent to the FreePats project thanks to the intermediation of Piotr Barz. The processing of the samples and creation of the sound bank has been made thanks to the work of michael02022 (https://github.com/michael02022).

Please visit FreePats web pages for updates and more content:
http://freepats.zenvoid.org/
